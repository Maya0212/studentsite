About the Cherry Blossom at the Chidori-ga-fuchi

The Chidorigafuchi Trench is one of the best places in Tokyo tosee the cherry blossoms in spring, from late March to early April.This 700-meter walk next to the Imperial Palace moat offersbeautiful scenery and great opportunities for discovery. During the popular cherry blossom season, the paths along the moat are colored pink by hundreds of cherry trees. The trees are also lit at night during the Chiyoda Sakura Cherry Blossom Festival. A little later in the year the surface of the water is almost completely covered by falling petals.
